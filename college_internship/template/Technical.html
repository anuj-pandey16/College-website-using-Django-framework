Institute is proud to introduce its Drama Club which is named “Lights, Camera, Action-Drama Club” or LCA drama club which is led by Mr Anukul Singh Bisht. LCA drama club’s mission is to bring together students of different backgrounds, and levels of experience for the purpose of producing and performing plays of all genres. LCA club members learn about acting techniques, proper auditioning, improvisational games, and much more. Institute understands that these days, it is important for students to make the most of their education and to learn valuable skills from the people around them. The Drama Club provides an opportunity for students to gain real-world experience that they would not be able to receive elsewhere. Through the club, students will have the chance to work with professionals, write and direct their own plays, and perform on stage. We believe that by providing a safe and creative atmosphere, students will be able to learn and grow in their abilities while having a great time. Institute believes that by giving students these experiences and skills, they will be fare better than others in the field of acting and drama. The Drama Club is open to anyone interested in taking part in theater and production and welcomes all levels of experience and encourage everyone to join, regardless of their skills. Club members are always excited to see new level of talent and enthusiasm that new members bring to the club.
