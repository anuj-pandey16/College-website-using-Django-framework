Event Management Club

Dr A.P.J Abdul Kalam Institute of Technology boasts a vibrant and diverse club that specializes in event management. The Event Management Club (EMC) is comprised of students from all walks of life, from all corners of the campus, from all disciplines. The Event management club (EMC) led by Dhatri Upadhayay is dedicated to providing students with the opportunity to take on a variety of events and have fun while learning about event management. Through the Event management club (EMC), students have the chance to organize and attend a variety of events and even partner with external organizations to host events. These events can be anything from career fairs, to conferences, to annual fests and more. The Event management club (EMC) also organizes smaller-scale events, such as lectures and workshops, to help members learn more about different aspects of event management. Members also have access to a variety of resources, such as templates and professional advice, to help them with their projects. The Event Management Club is a great place for students to engage with each other and share ideas. It is also a great way to make friends and gain valuable experience. The EMC provides students with an opportunity to learn more about event management, hone their skills, and create lasting memories.
